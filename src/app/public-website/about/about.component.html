DGB Commerce is an initiative by Leroy Gerrits, a full-stack web developer and former business owner from the Netherlands. Being disappointed by the lack of utility and real life use cases for Digibyte, and having many years of experience with e-commerce solutions and SAAS platforms, he put two and two together and decided to create more utility for Digibyte in the form of DGB Commerce.

While there are existing means for merchants to offer their products and get paid with Digibyte, most of them exist in the form of plugins/add-ons/components for third-party software, charge hefty fees, are cumbersome to use and/or require at least some technical know-how to implement. With DGB Commerce, that threshold is gone and merchants can fully focus on their products and sales once more.

Intuitive interface

A state of the art control panel makes handling your catalog, customers and sales a breeze. Our user interface requires no technical knowledge whatsoever. Your store can be up and running in a matter of minutes.

Transparant

Our code repositories are public on GitHub, all of the transactions to and from our wallets will be elaborated in public ledgers and we will continuously report on our progress through social media outlets and our News section.
