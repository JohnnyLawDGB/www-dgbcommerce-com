About DGB Commerce

DGB Commerce is an initiative by Leroy Gerrits, a full-stack web developer and former business owner from the Netherlands with many years of experience building e-commerce solutions and SAAS platforms. Being disappointed by the scarcity of utility and real life use cases for DigiByte he decided to put two and two together and create DGB Commerce.

While there are existing means for merchants to offer their products and get paid with DigiByte, most of them exist in the form of plugins/add-ons/components for third-party software, charge hefty fees, are cumbersome to use and/or require at least some technical know-how to implement. With DGB Commerce, those thresholds are gone and merchants can fully focus on their products and sales once more.

Our mission is not only to supply merchants a toolbox to easily create a web shop to sell goods and services, but also to raise awareness about DigiByte, its transaction speed and suitability as a peer to peer payment method.

Intuitive interface

A state of the art control panel makes handling your product catalog, customers and sales a breeze. Our user interface requires no technical knowledge whatsoever. Your store can be up and running in a matter of minutes.

Transparent

Our code repositories are public on GitHub, all of our expenses, withdrawals and donations are visible in our Financial statement and we will continuously report on our progress through social media outlets and our News section.
