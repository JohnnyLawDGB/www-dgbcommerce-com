Financial Statement

We value transparency. All of our expenses and profits will be documented in this financial statement. DGB Commerce makes use of 2 public DigiByte wallets: a regular wallet which is used to cover expenses and receive donations, and a transactions wallet which is used for all automated transactions that happen when consumers buy products and goods from merchants using our platform.

On regular intervals, DGB Commerce will transfer Digibyte from the automated transaction wallet to the regular wallet. In the event of such a transfer or a donation, a Debit entry will be added to this financial statement.
